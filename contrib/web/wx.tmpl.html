Data is from the SotonMet WAP feed and adjusted for the wind sensor height.

The Netley Marsh data is obtained from a number of 1-wire weather sensors in our garden. You can find out more information about the hardware and software used (Linux, 1-wire, Sqlite), on the technical description page.

There are also some pictures of the sensors and Daria's greenhouse in the image gallery.

Our garden is surrounded by trees, therefore we do not have an anemometer; as we sail at Weston Sailing Club on Southampton Water, it is convenient to use the SotonMet WAP feed for wind information. The wind speed is normalised from the value measured at 53m to a more normal 10m for display.
